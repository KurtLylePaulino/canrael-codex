# The Canrael Codex — Suno Music Prompts

Instrumental ambient beds, one per region. In **Suno**: turn the **Instrumental**
toggle **ON**, paste the line into the **Style** box, and leave lyrics empty.
"Textless choir" = wordless vocal pads (no words) — remove that phrase for a fully
choirless take. Designed as quiet, loopable background music.

When rendered, save each track under `assets/audio/` using the filename in the table
at the bottom, and the per-region ambient music can be wired in (off by default,
crossfading to match the selected region).

---

## ✦ All Voices — *The Canrael Codex (Main Theme)*
